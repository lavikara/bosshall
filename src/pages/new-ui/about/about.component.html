Bosshalls is a live streaming platform that allows radio/non-radio presenters to display contents that represent the message of their broadcast during sports, entertainment review shows.

Bosshalls targets categories like

  • Sports

  • Entertainment

  • Variety

Ideology

Yes, radio content can be seen more than just listening and imagining. Even though radio presenters currently can be seen live on screen across social platforms while broadcasting, Extreme Visual Broadcast is a new norm and slogan that can go a long way in transforming their broadcasting experience and add way more value to the presenter's career,audience and work-flow.

E.V.B

E.V.B- Extreme Visual Broadcast was coined at bosshall to illustrate a new broadcasting format in the audio-media industry where OAPs goes extreme from displaying their studio cameras on-screen to displaying videos and pictures that represents the message of their broadcast as they analyze them.
